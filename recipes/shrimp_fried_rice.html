Ingredients

  • 10oz raw ribeye steak
  • 3 inch butter
  • 3 strands thyme
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Put heat on high
  2. Add olive oil
  3. Add steak
  4. Add butter and thyme
  5. Flip steak after 3 1/2 minutes
  6. Cook for another 3 1/2 minutes and you are done!
